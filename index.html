I decided I wanted to work in pharmaceutical manufacturing!

After graduating from Clemson University with a Chemical Engineering degree with a concentration in Biomolecular Engineering, I had a hard time breaking into the pharma game. My first job was working for an OE BMW polymer bumper supplier in the paint quality department. There we molded, painted, and assembled the external polymer pieces for multiple BMW SUVs. After that, I worked for a major tire manufacturer where I was in charge of ensuring quality cures of tires and also trying to figure out how to reduce the cure times.

While both of those jobs were great, I still was always looking for my shot to enter the pharmaceutical field. In March 2022, it finally happened! I am now a process engineer over two commercial pharmaceutical drugs and hope to stay in this business until I retire.
